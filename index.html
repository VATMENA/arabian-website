Welcome to the Arabian vACC

#ClearVision #ClearMission

#ClearVision

To become the leading vACC on the VATSIM network, known for its high-quality ATC, and enjoyable pilot experience.

#ClearMission

To provide the most realistic simulation possible of day-to-day aviation operations throughout the Arabian Gulf, remaining faithful to the real-world procedures.
The Arabian vACC (Virtual Area Control Centre) is a vibrant community of virtual controllers and pilots who are part of the VATSIM network. The community is comprised of a multicultural membership base, with virtual controllers and pilots from over thirty different countries, many of whom are real-world aviation professionals with a sound understanding of operations unique to each FIR and are ready to welcome you to the virtual skies!

Frequently Asked Questions

If you are part of the Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) region, you may send an email to VATMENA membership with your name and VATSIM ID stating your intention to join the Arabian vACC.
If your region is not EMEA, submit a region change request on myVATSIM and then send an email to VATMENA membership with your name and VATSIM ID stating your intention to join the Arabian vACC.
You can check your assigned region by logging in to Oryx and clicking on my profile on the top right and checking your user profile.

Our front page displays all our upcoming events where your can expect ATC so check back regularly as we update our events page! You can also join our Discord where we post regular event updates and other community announcements.

You can join our community discord here.

If you are a home member, first ensure that you have join the Arabian vACC by following the steps described above. Once you have done that, submit a support ticket on Oryx and our staff will guide you through the process to commence your training. If you wish to visit the Arabian vACC, first ensure that you meet the minimum requirements as defined in our membership policy, then submit a support ticket on Oryx and our staff will provide you with guidance.

All training material links are on our Discord or on Oryx under the training documents section on the left-hand side.
